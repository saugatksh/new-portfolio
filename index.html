About Me

I’m a passionate developer with a strong foundation in Web and App Development. Holding a Bachelor’s degree in Information and Technology, I thrive on creating digital solutions that blend functionality with innovation.

My expertise lies in the Odoo Framework, where I’ve developed and customized ERP systems such as School ERP, Payroll Management, and Agriculture ERP, among others — each designed to enhance efficiency and streamline operations.

I’m currently engaged in multiple website and software design projects focused on scalability, user experience, and modern design principles. Some of my notable works include an Online Bus Reservation System, an Online Parking Spot App (Flutter), and an Online Movie Recommendation System.

Fueled by curiosity and creativity, I take pride in delivering high-quality, reliable, and innovative solutions that make a tangible impact. I believe every line of code should tell a story — one of efficiency, functionality, and elegance.
